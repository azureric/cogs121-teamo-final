Got the Feels is a web application that displays data about Anxiety Disorder and allows you, the user, to interpret the information and understand the prevalence of anxiety in society.

Anxiety rates have increased by 51% between 2006 and 2016 with little effort to minimize cases compared to other health issues such as lung cancer.

Now that you have seen a glimpse of the statistics, you just made the first step towards confronting the stigma of mental health.
